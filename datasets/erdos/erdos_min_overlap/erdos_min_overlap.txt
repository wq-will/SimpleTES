Find a step function h: [0, 2] → [0, 1] that **minimizes** the overlap integral:

$$C_5 = \\max_k \\int h(x)(1 - h(x+k)) dx$$

1. h(x) ∈ [0, 1] for all x
2. ∫₀² h(x) dx = 1

With dx = 2.0 / n_points:
- 0 ≤ h[i] ≤ 1 for all i
- sum(h) * dx = 1 (equivalently: sum(h) == n_points / 2 exactly)
